PARINAMA in Simple Words
What it does: You give it a bad/mediocre prompt → it makes it better, automatically, step by step.

Think of it like this:

Imagine you write a rough email to your boss. PARINAMA would:

Read your email and grade it (0-100) on 5 things:

Is it clear?
Is it specific enough?
Is it actionable (does it tell what to do)?
Is it concise (not too wordy)?
Is it creative/engaging?
Find the weakest part — say clarity is only 40/100

Pick a fix strategy — "Let's CLARIFY this" (one of 6 strategies)

Rewrite the prompt using AI to fix that weakness

Grade the new version — clarity went from 40 → 72

Repeat until the score is above 90 or it's tried 5-7 times

Example:

You type: "write about dogs"
After evolution: "Write a 500-word engaging blog post about the top 3 health benefits of owning a dog, backed by recent studies, targeting first-time pet owners considering adoption"
The tech behind it:

Uses 3 free AI models (Groq, Gemini, Ollama) — if one fails, it switches to the next
WebSocket streams results live — you watch the AI rewriting in real-time, character by character
Visual dashboard shows radar charts, score graphs, and an evolution tree of how your prompt improved
In one line: It's a prompt gym — your prompts go in weak, come out strong. 💪🧬
